Bluebell

The flower, bluebell, has beautiful bulbous, which ensures a profusion of color ranging from deep purple to pinks, whites, and blues grow on summer seasons.

Chrysanthemum

Chrysanthemum flowers, frequently called mums of chrysanths, are the most popular flowers in the world after the rose flowers.

Dahlia

Dahlia is a flower associated with various meanings, like reports of listening to signals of warning. This flower used to show some elegance, creativity, change, dignity.

Daisy

The scientific name for daisy is Bellis perennis, who comes from the family Asteraceae. This flower can be grown generally in winters, and the pollination goes in late autumns.

Iris

Iridaceae, also called iris, is a flower that is used in medicines. It aimed to be bruised with wine and purged major efficiency.

Jasmin

Jasmine, a name of a flower that smells very nice, comes from the olive family. The color of this flower is mostly white, and it grows in the branches without tendrils.

Lavender

The lavender flower is known for its beautiful color and beautiful fragrance. This is also called Lavandula from the mint family. It comes in many colors like purple, pale lilac, deep royal purple, pink, red, green, white, etc.

Lily

The flower lily is a herbaceous flower growing from bulbs. These flowers are significant in culture and literature in the world.

Marigold

Marigold flowers are also called Target erecta. This flower is generally used for some ritual purpose or decoration.

Poppy

The scientific name for the flower named poppy is Papaverales. This is a herbaceous plant with a lot of colors. Its species, Papaver Somniferum, is the narcotic drug opium.

Rose

Rose is the name of the flower, a type of flowering shrub. The name Rose comes from the Latin word, Rosa. This flower is a symbol of love and friendship. These flowers are available in various colors like red, black, yellow, purple, pink, blue, orange, etc., and have a beautiful fragrance.

Tulip

Tulip flowers are cup-shaped flowers having several colors and a large green stem. This flower comes from the family of the flower lily. This flower is mostly used in decorating bookies.
